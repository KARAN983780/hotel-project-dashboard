# hotel-project-dashboard
 Hotel Revenue Analysis Dashboard (Excel)  This project is an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze hotel performance across multiple dimensions such as revenue, bookings, customer demographics, and city-wise contribution. 
The dashboard helps identify key revenue drivers, top-performing cities, and customer behavior patterns to support data-driven business decisions.  


 Key Features 
Monthly Revenue Analysis with trend comparison  City-wise Revenue Concentration to identify major revenue-generating locations  Traveler vs Corporate Customer Analysis  Gender-based Booking Distribution  Age Group Contribution Analysis  Dynamic Filters (Slicers) for Gender, Month, and City  Revenue displayed in millions (M) for better readability 


 Insights Generated  
Revenue is primarily driven by traveler customers  A few major cities contribute most of the total revenue  Adult customers form the largest revenue-contributing group  Male customers contribute slightly more bookings than female customers  Revenue remains consistent throughout the year with seasonal peaks
